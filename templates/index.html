About Us

ROOMZY & Roomrent Management System

The project entitled "ROOMZY" is a web-based application that allows users to find homes and rooms according to their requirements, and lets them advertise their vacant rooms or houses using web browsers.

Currently, customers need to visit multiple places in search of rooms, and house owners must contact several people to find reliable tenants. With ROOMZY, users can search online and directly contact the house owners, as all details are provided in each listing.

This saves valuable time for both seekers and renters. The website is built using a 3-tier architecture: frontend, backend, and database.
